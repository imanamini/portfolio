02 · The Problem

QA was the firewall
for every library release.

A one-line change inside ngx-button could ripple through every product. Without automation, the only net was manual regression across every consumer app — on every release.

01 · BLAST RADIUS

One change, N apps

Every library is consumed by multiple production apps. A bug in ngx-utils could surface in half of Digipay.

02 · HUMAN COST

QA regression per release

Every release forced QA to re-run a manual sweep across consumer screens. Slow, expensive, and error-prone.

03 · DEV CHILL

Developers stopped refactoring

The safest change was no change. Tech debt grew because nobody wanted to trigger another full QA pass.
